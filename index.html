"The task of making data meaningful and useful to ordinary citizens who can benefit from it is not easy...
There is more to designing open data than just making it searchable, discoverable, available, and presenting it attractively"(OECD (2018), Open Government Data Report)
Open data portals are a valuable resource, providing access to a wide range of datasets. However, .
Many users are unaware of data portals, as search engines do not typically crawl these portals or index the datasets within them. As a result, these portals and their valuable data remain largely hidden from the public
Say hello to ODDS!
ODDS provides a new way to make data accessible, searchable, and usable. It allows users to ask questions using Large Language Models, and it searches any portal it is installed on. Additionally, it eliminates the need for time-consuming downloads and extensive analysis. ODDS searches within the dataset and assists users in finding the answers they seek. If the required data is unavailable, ODDS offers suggestions for alternative datasets that may be useful.
Scan Open Data Catalogues and Websites
Process, Analyze and Index the datasets
Use an AI interface to answer complex user questions
